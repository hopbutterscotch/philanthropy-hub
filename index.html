Orientation

Start here before planning events or editing records.

Purpose of the Role

The philanthropy chairman plans, organizes, records, and improves the chapter's philanthropy work. The role is not just about running events. It is also about leaving behind a better system for the next chairman.

First 7 Days
  • Meet with the outgoing chairman.
  • Find the current Drive folder, spreadsheets, templates, and receipts.
  • Review any past event records that exist.
  • Confirm expectations with chapter leadership.
First 30 Days
  • Build the semester philanthropy calendar.
  • Choose likely events and partners.
  • Create a working budget.
  • Assign responsibilities early.
After Each Event
  • Record what happened.
  • Save receipts and files.
  • Write what worked and what should change.
  • Update this hub before the details are forgotten.

Raffle

  • Confirm prize and rules.
  • Create a sales tracker.
  • Promote with a clear deadline.
  • Record winner, revenue, costs, and lessons learned.

Tournament

  • Reserve location.
  • Create bracket and rules.
  • Assign check-in, referee, and cleanup roles.
  • Record teams, revenue, costs, and issues.

Business Outreach

  • Build a target list.
  • Use one clear sponsorship ask.
  • Track follow-ups.
  • Send a thank-you and impact summary.

Golf Ball Drop

  • Confirm location and safety requirements.
  • Track ticket and ball numbers carefully.
  • Assign judges and recorder.
  • Save final results and process notes.
